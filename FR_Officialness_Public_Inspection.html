      • Waiver for Certain Defense Items Produced in the United Kingdom

        A Notice by the Defense Acquisition Regulations System on 02/14/2013

        The Under Secretary of Defense is waiving the statutory limitation of 10 U.S.C. 2534 for certain defense items produced in the United Kingdom. The law limits DoD procurement of certain items to sources in the national technology and industrial base. The waiver will permit procurement of enumerated items from sources in the UK, unless otherwise restricted by statute.
